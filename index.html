Our company was founded on the basis that everyone should have equal access to quality, delicious donuts no matter their dietary restrictions. Our donuts are free from the eight major allergens including milk, eggs, fish, shellfish, tree nuts, peanuts, wheat, and soybeans. Everyone is welcome at Fred the Baker Baking Company! We have created this virtual donut clicker game so that everyone can simulate the creation of delicious donuts no matter where they are in the world!
