The Company

Our company was founded on the basis that everyone should have equal access to quality, delicious donuts no matter their dietary restrictions. Our donuts are free from the eight major allergens including milk, eggs, fish, shellfish, tree nuts, peanuts, wheat, and soybeans. Everyone is welcome at Fred the Baker Baking Company!
